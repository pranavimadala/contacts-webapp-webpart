Demand Response

Participate in our Reduce for Rewards Program to receive $5 credits for every kilowatt hour of electricity you save during a high demand event!

High demand events usually occur only a few times every year,generally on hot days when lots of households use increased electricity to keep cool. this high demand can cause risks to the energy grid,including blackouts.

Ny opting to participate in Reduce to Rewards Program and voluntarily reduce your energy consumption,you can help to reduce these risks and get rewarded for your efforts!
